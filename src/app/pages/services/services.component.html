ICT Services

00
Accounts and Authentication

CUT ICTC ensures that only authorized access to Institutional Information and ICT resources begins with proper account and authentication management. ICTC Account and Authentication Management Services as guided by the ICT Policy (Information Security Policy), defines the pre-requisites regardingnew user onboarding, account access, including but not limited to: developing and changing passphrases (passwords), multifactor authentication and maintaining and deleting accounts to minimize cyber risks.

01
Administrative Systems

Software Application packages (Platforms) that are used by the University for day to day running of operations (Online Filing and Recording), these Systems include the Admissions andAcademic Records System, Financial packages, Human Resources Management Systems, Online Databases for Research and Institutional repositories.

02
Teaching & Learning

This quality service is offered in collaboration with the Academy of Teaching and Learning. The ICTC provisions projectors and interactive screens for teaching and learning.

In addition the Centre provides a Hardware and Software Infrastructure for Virtual Learning (Digital Learning) by integrating Moodle with the Academic Registry data for excellence in providing Education 5.0 service.

03
Chat, Video & Collaboration

"Contemporary administration, learning and teaching requires synchronous collaboration through online and virtual platforms. The ICTC works on video conferencing tools (cameras, headsets, earphones) and solutions (eg BigBlueButton, Jitsi, Skype, Google meet etc.) designed to make it easier to work remotely/virtually with others to get things done.

04
Computers & Software

The ICTC facilitates sustainable procurement and disposal of Computers and Software, Hardware replacement and replacement, software licensing on behalf of the university community.

05
Email & Calendars

21st century working enables setting up of online calendars and important notification configuration, this promotes a culture of working together as stakeholders through email, online scheduling and ubiquitous access to your calendar of events. Through cloud hosted emails stakeholders are able to do online co-editing of documents

06
Cyber Security

Critical mission University data (infrastructure and software) needs protection from manipulation and unauthorized access. The Cyber Security Desk strategizes for Business Continuity and Disaster recovery through following procedures of backup as guided by the Backup and Business Continuity Policy. These services have a dedicated taskforce.

07
Phones & Mobile

The 21st Century has witnessed a surge in utilization of Mobile Technologies and Digital phones (VOIP Systems). The infrastructure that supports quality and flawless service requires maintenance. The ICTC has qualified technocrats to service office phones, mobile phones and tablet PCs.

08

09
Wired & Wireless Networks

The Fourth Industrial Revolution and 21st Century buzzword is “Internet”, CUT ICTC provisions both Wired and Wireless at the main and sub campuses.

Connection to the Internet is provisioned through the Authorized Access Policy and Bring Your Own Device (BOYD) Policy.
