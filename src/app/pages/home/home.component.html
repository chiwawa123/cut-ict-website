Welcome to Information and Communication Technology Department at Chinhoyi University of Technology (CUT ICT). CUT ICT makes available a wide array of technology resources to support innovative teaching, learning, research, community service and administration. The Information and Communication Technology Department operates under the direction of the Executive ICT Director who reports directly to the Vice Chancellor and is a member of the Computer Society of Zimbabwe. The CUT ICT Department strives to offer the best of 21st Century support in ubiquitous, integrated, and fully engaging digital learning experiences. We take an engaging, proactive and collaborative approach to deliver innovative, resilient, reliable, and sustainable information and communication technologies that optimize user satisfaction and desired outcomes. We provide and will continue to provide sector leading cutting edge digital solutions and services for the benefit of the entire university academic and administrative community in the following major functional areas: Academic Computing, Administrative Computing, End-User Training and Support, Communications and Networking, and Systems Administration and Operation. We are a customer-centric, service oriented and responsive department that is highly regarded by faculty, students and staff in the University.
