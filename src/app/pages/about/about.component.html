About CUT ICT

The Chinhoyi University of Technology Information and Communication Technology (ICT) is a central Computing Centre for computing technology operations, a strategic partner to many areas of the university mandated to develop and maintain policies and technological systems that support teaching and learning. Born out of Innovation and results, the ICT Department is currently equipped with both physical and human resources to provide high quality information services and systems across the University. The department provides customer centered technologically innovative computing and telecommunication facilities to enable and transformative teaching, research, extension, consultancy and administrative activities for all (administrative, support and academic) units in the University. The department is empowered to exploit ICT to its full potential thereby assisting the University in elevating its position to a center of academic excellence and to support the realization of the strategic objectives of the University.

Our Vision

To deliver dynamic, digitally vigilant, and value driven ICT services powering creativity and innovation in the university

Our Mission

To provide a modern, integrated, resilient, quality, secure and sustainable technological environment that ensures innovative, efficient teaching, learning, research and administration process.
